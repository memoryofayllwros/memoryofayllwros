## Featured Projects

### [Material Provenance & Custody System](https://github.com/memoryofayllwros/material-provenance-custody-system)
*Sole architect & developer · Virpluz Limited (Internal Trial) · 2024–2026*

Enterprise supply chain tracking platform for construction materials — full chain of custody from origin through final delivery.

- **Event-sourced audit log** (Kafka + CQRS + Outbox pattern) guarantees at-least-once delivery and full provenance reconstruction at any point in time
- **Hyperledger Fabric** on-chain anchoring provides a tamper-evident custody record independent of the application database
- **Multi-agent AI system** with three execution modes: Guidance (Q&A), Data Check (structured query), and Data Analysis (parallel multi-intent orchestration)
- **Structured Intent Generator (SIG)** compiles natural language into analytical plans constrained by a METRIC\_REGISTRY — eliminating LLM hallucination of schema fields
- **Distillation pipeline** captures full teacher model (Claude Sonnet) reasoning traces for future domain-specific fine-tuning

`Python` `FastAPI` `React` `Kafka` `MongoDB` `LangGraph` `Hyperledger Fabric` `Docker` `CQRS` `Event Sourcing`
